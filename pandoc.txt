# Team Onboarding Plan (has Mermaid diagrams — two-step process)
# Step 1: mmdc pre-renders the 4 mermaid diagrams to SVG files and produces a processed .md
# Step 2: pandoc converts the processed .md (with SVG image references) to PDF
# This avoids relying on JavaScript execution in wkhtmltopdf entirely.
mmdc -i AIBOS_Uganda_Team_Onboarding_Plan.md -o AIBOS_Uganda_Team_Onboarding_Plan_processed.md -t neutral -b white -w 1000
pandoc AIBOS_Uganda_Team_Onboarding_Plan_processed.md -o AIBOS_Uganda_Team_Onboarding_Plan.pdf --pdf-engine=wkhtmltopdf --standalone -t html5 --no-highlight -V margin-top=22mm -V margin-bottom=22mm -V margin-left=25mm -V margin-right=25mm
